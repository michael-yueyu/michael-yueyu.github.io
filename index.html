Dr. Yue Yu is currently a Research Assistant Professor in Department of Land surveying and Geo-Informatics at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University and Research Affiliate at the Otto Poon Charitable Foundation Smart Cities Research Institute led by Prof. John SHI. Before joining PolyU as a Research Assistant Professor, he was a Postdoctoral fellow at LSGI of PolyU.

Dr. Yu received the B.Sc. degree and M.Sc. Degree from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2015 and 2018, respectively. After that, he received dual Ph.D. degrees in both Wuhan University and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2021 and 2022, supervised by Prof. John SHI and Prof. Ruizhi CHEN. He has published more than 30 SCI papers, and received more than 400 citations in Google Scholar, H-Index researches 11; He is also serving as the reviewers of more than 20 SCI journals including “IEEE TII”, “IEEE TMC”, “IEEE TWC”,“IEEE IotJ”, “JAG” and has applied for more than 15 patents and software copyrights. He won the Champions of IPIN International Indoor Positioning Competition twice in 2018 and 2020, and won the nomination of excellent Ph.D. graduation thesis in Wuhan University in 2021.

Dr. Yu 's research interests include Urban Informatics, Seamless Positioning and Navigation, Mobile Mapping, and Geo-spatial Data Analytics.
